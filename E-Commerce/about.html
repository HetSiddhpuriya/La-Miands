Our Story

Growing Green, One Plant at a Time

Founded in 2020, Urban Jungle Co. started as a small passion project by two plant lovers who believed that everyone deserves to experience the joy and tranquility that plants bring to a space.

What began as a tiny greenhouse has blossomed into one of the most trusted online plant retailers. We partner directly with sustainable nurseries to bring you the healthiest, most vibrant plants — each one hand-picked and inspected before it reaches your doorstep.

Our commitment goes beyond just selling plants. We provide expert care guides, a 30-day plant health guarantee, and responsive customer support to ensure your green companions thrive.
