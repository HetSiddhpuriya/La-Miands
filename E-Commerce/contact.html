Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our plants and service

How do you ship plants safely?

Every plant is carefully packaged in eco-friendly, breathable packaging with secure root wrapping. We use insulated boxes for temperature-sensitive plants and include moisture retention materials to keep them hydrated during transit.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day health guarantee. If your plant arrives damaged or doesn't survive within the first 30 days despite following our care instructions, we'll replace it free of charge.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat shipping rate of $5.99 applies.

Can I get plant care advice?

Absolutely! Every plant comes with a detailed care guide. You can also reach out to our plant care specialist through email or the contact form above for personalized advice.
